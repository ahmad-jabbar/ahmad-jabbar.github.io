Ahmad Jabbar

I am a PhD student at Stanford Linguistics. I am advised by Prof. Christopher Potts. I work on natural-language semantics and formal pragmatics. My current projects focus on compositionality and discourse structure. Other interests include cs theory, psycholinguistics, and rational analysis. My previous degrees are in logic and philosophy. Overall, I am motivated by a desire to make explanations clearer and simpler.
