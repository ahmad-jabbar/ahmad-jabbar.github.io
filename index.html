I am a PhD student at Stanford Linguistics. I am primarily interested in natural language meaning; its useful representations and processing. My current projects focus on compositionality, discourse structure, and conditionals. In my research, I seek to understand things deeply via math. Sometimes, it's alegbraic, sometimes statistical.

I am affiliated with the Stanford NLP Group and the ALPS lab. My previous degrees are in logic and philosophy. I also house a keen interest in computation theory.
