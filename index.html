Ahmad Jabbar

jabbar@stanford.edu

I am a PhD candidate at Stanford Linguistics. I work on semantics, nlp, logic, and cognitive psychology. I am advised by Christopher Potts and Cleo Condoravdi. I also work closely with Thomas Icard.

In my semantics work, I build symbolic models for discourse particles, clause-types, modals, and conditionals. On the computational side, I work on compositional generalization and interpretability. As an undergrad, I read Philosophy.

With Cleo Condoravdi, I co-organize the Construction of Meaning workshop. With Jasper Jian, I co-organize a group at the intersection of language modeling and linguistic theory.
