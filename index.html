I am a PhD student at Stanford Linguistics, advised by Prof. Christopher Potts. I work on natural-language semantics and formal pragmatics. My current projects focus on compositionality and discourse structure.
My other interests are cs theory, psycholinguistics, and rational analysis. Overall, using math and computational tools, I seek to explain how humans compute meaning.
I am a member of the Stanford NLP Group and the ALPS lab. My previous degrees are in logic and philosophy.

Feel free to reach out if you're interested in any of the above topics and don't know where to start. I am happy to chat.
